Rules

Survive as long as you can.

As time goes on the difficulty will increase

If you are hit by a target you will die and the game will end

Destroy as many targets as you can for points

Targets can only be destroyed by the same color projectile

Controls

Use the mouse to move around

Use w a s d keys to shoot in all directions

Press w key to shoot a red projectile

Press a key to shoot a purple projectile

Press s key to shoot a green projectile

Press d key to shoot a yellow projectile
